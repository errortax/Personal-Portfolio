Analytics & Platform Analysis

Comparative evaluations that inform platform choices and migration plans.

Scope

Compared Snowflake, Databricks, and lakehouse patterns for data warehousing needs; compared Mixpanel, Amplitude, and Heap for product analytics.

Approach

  • Defined evaluation criteria: ingestion latency, query performance, cost model, operational overhead, ecosystem integrations.
  • Built prototype ingestion and sample dashboards for each platform to measure time-to-insight.

Findings

Snowflake offers excellent SQL performance for BI workloads; Databricks shines for ML/ETL heavy teams. Mixpanel provides straightforward product analytics with strong composability for funnels, while Amplitude excels at behavioral cohorting and cross-device stitching.

Recommendations

Choose based on primary workload: BI-first organizations -> Snowflake + dbt; ML-first organizations -> Databricks. For product metrics, prioritize event quality and instrumentation before committing to a vendor.
